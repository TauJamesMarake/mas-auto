About MAS Auto

Our Story

More Than Just
A Workshop

MAS Auto was founded on a simple belief: every vehicle deserves professional care, and every customer deserves honest, transparent service. Based in Polokwane's Ladanna district, we have are a growing small repair shop in the region. We aim to beacome the most respected automotive service centre.

Our team of skilled, certified technicians brings together decades of combined experience across a wide spectrum of vehicle makes and models, from German precision engineering to Japanese reliability. We invest continuously in modern diagnostic equipment and training to stay ahead of evolving automotive technology.

Whether you're bringing in your daily driver for routine maintenance, seeking a full engine overhaul, or looking to upgrade your vehicle's performance and audio system, MAS Auto is your trusted partner on the road.
